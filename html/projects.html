FATSealOS v1.0.1



FATSealOS is a minature operating system kernel (soon to be a full OS) developed to boot with GRUB2. The kernel is still in early stages, so it does not provide the full range of capabilities that you would expect, but hey... at least it boots :). More features will be added on a regular (but very sporadic) basis. Feedback on discord or e-mail.
